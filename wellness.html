Why live with a damaged gut microbiome when you could fix it?

Inside of you there’s an invisible ecosystem of trillions of microbes. Among the most important is a group we call the Foundation Guild™. The bacteria in your Foundation Guild™ are central to your overall health—they produce beneficial substances that nurture your body and boost your immunity. Akin to tall trees in forest, the Foundation Guild™ also supports a robust gut ecosystem that is suppressive to harmful bacteria. Our Foundation Guild™ Wellness Program aims to identify, protect, restore and maintain your Foundation Guild™, helping you live a longer and healthier life.

Step 1: Guild:Quest Foundation Guild™ Analysis

Our Guild:Quest Analysis can identify the Foundation Guild™ bacteria in every person's gut, no matter how small of a percentage they are in the beginning.

The Guild:Quest fecal sample collection kit is easy to use. Follow the simple instructions in the kit and mail the sample back to us. We will analyze the sample and send you a report about your Foundation Guild™.

Step 2: Guild:Plus Foundation Guild™ Nutrition

Our Guild:Plus Microbiome Nutrition contains the necessary nutrients and protectants to support the Foundation Guild™ bacteria in healthy individuals. Take three scoops each day, and you will soon notice the difference.

Mix the powder in water and drink it directly, or add it into your favorite smoothie blend. The formula is so versatile that you can even make bread, muffins, waffles, or pancakes with it!

Step 3: Guild:Save Foundation Guild™ Bio-banking

Just like having a back-up copy of your most important files, you should also preserve a copy of your Foundation Guild™.

Once we confirm that your Foundation Guild™ has recovered, you will receive a Guild:Save Bio-banking kit to collect another sample. As soon as your sample arrives to our lab, we will isolate your Foundation Guild™ bacteria and put them into long term storage. If, in the future, your Foundation Guild™ is lost, we can help you regain your original Foundation Guild™
