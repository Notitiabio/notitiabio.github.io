Humans are superorganisms

We are superorganisms that are made up of our own genome, and our microbiome, which is the millions of microbes living on and inside our bodies.

The microbiome is an integral part of the human body, performing metabolic functions that are vital to our health.

Gut microbiome is a complex ecosystem within our bodies

Within the 2 kg (4.4 lbs) of microbes inside the gut microbiome, there are more than 1000 species of bacteria that are either beneficial, neutral, or harmful to the body.

The complexity and density of bacteria in the gut is such that it can be called our microbial Amazon Jungle.

Foundation Guild™ is the most important group within the gut microbiome

Foundation Guild™, discovered by Dr. Liping Zhao from Rutgers University, is responsible for structuring a healthy gut microbiome and keeping it stable.

These microbes are like the tall trees of the rain forest, and if they are gone, the entire gut ecosystem would collapse.

Foundation Guild™ fights pathogens that are driving diseases

Opposite to the Foundation Guild™ is a group of harmful bacteria called the pathogen guild. These dangerous bacteria are responsible for creating harmful toxins that can damage the body and aggravate diseases.

The Foundation Guild™ and the pathogen guild are two armies that are always at war with each other , the outcome of this fight would dictate whether if our microbiome is healthy or diseased.

Promoting the Foundation Guild™ will bring-back the healthy gut microbiome

The army that wins is the one that you support, and Notitia has developed products and programs that can promote health by protecting and/or restoring the Foundation Guild™ of the gut microbiome.
