NBT-NM108 Covid-19 in T2D & Prediabetes
Product overview
NBT-NM108 is an investigational, oral, high-fiber nutrient-mix drink for the treatment of COVID-19 and other respiratory viral infections in patients with type 2 diabetes (T2D) and prediabetes. The FDA has allowed the product to be tested in a phase 2a clinical trial to assess its efficacy in diabetic and prediabetic COVID-19 patients.
