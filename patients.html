Restoring the Permanently Lost Foundation Guild™ is Possible.

Notitia's revolutionary Foundation Guild™ Microbiome R&D Platform allows us to develop unique and effective treatments for various chronic and acute complications.

Bacterial Medicine (BM306)

A pill that contains the Foundation Guild™ bacteria. This drug will replenish the missing or extinct Foundation Guild™ bacteria in the patient's gut microbiome.

Nutritional Medicine (NM108)

A nutrient formula that can promote the growth of either the existing or replenished Foundation Guild™ bacteria in a patients gut microbiome. This formula is tailored to patients with a severely damaged or extremely fragile gut microbiome.
