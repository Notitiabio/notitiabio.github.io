Why live with a damaged gut microbiome when you could fix it?

We have found that most individuals have a damaged Foundation Guild™ in their gut microbiome. If left unsupported, the pathogen guilds can overtake the gut ecosystem.

We have developed a comprehensive strategy that uses a three-step program to analyze, repair, and preserve your Foundation Guild™ bacteria.

Step 1: Analyze your Existing Foundation Guild™

Our Foundation Guild™ Analysis (FG-A) test kit can discover the novel Foundation Guild™ bacteria in every person's gut, no matter how small of a percentage they are in the beginning.

The test kit is also easy to use. Simply follow the instructions in each kit and after mailing the sample back to us, you will receive your results.

Step 2: Repair your Damaged Foundation Guild™

Our Foundation Guild™ Microbiome Dietary Supplement (MDS163) contains the necessary nutrients and protectants to support the Foundation Guild™ bacteria in healthy individuals. Take three scoops each day, and you will feel an immediate and dramatic difference.

You can mix the powder in water and drink it directly, or you can add it into your favorite smoothie blend. The formula is so versatile that you can even make bread, muffins, waffles, or pancakes with it!

Step 3: Preserve your Recovered Foundation Guild™

Just like having a back-up copy of your most important files, you should also have a copy of your Foundation Guild™.

Once we confirm that your Foundation Guild™ has recovered, you will receive a Foundation Guild™ Bio-banking (FG-B) kit to collect another sample. As soon as your sample arrives to our lab, we will prepare your Foundation Guild™ for cryogenic storage.
