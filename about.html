About Us

Notitia ("NO-TEE-SHA") means "data" in Latin. We are a next-generation microbiome company that unlocks the gut microbiome's data to develop innovative products and therapies for patients and the general public.

Our mission is to promote health by protecting and/or restoring the Foundation Guild of the gut microbiome.

We believe that we can maximize everyone's healthspan by promoting the gut microbiome to the healthiest state.
